## What this is

I'm a Performance and Power Engineer on the SoC Architecture Performance
team at Arm in San Diego. The work is **pre-silicon** — quantifying
latency, throughput, energy, and area impacts of compute subsystems
across the hardware/software stack so architectural trade-offs can be
made before there's any silicon to measure.

## The shape of the work

- Workload characterization with hardware counters and custom telemetry.
- Building and maintaining performance evaluation and automation
  frameworks in Python and C++.
- Integrating performance data into CI for regression detection.
- Communicating performance insights to architecture, microarchitecture,
  and systems teams to influence product roadmaps.
