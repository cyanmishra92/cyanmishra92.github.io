Seeker is a project focused on synergizing data from mobile devices (like smartphones) and energy-harvesting wearable sensors for robust Human Activity Recognition (HAR). By combining the rich contextual information from mobiles with the continuous, low-power sensing from wearables, Seeker aims to achieve higher accuracy and reliability in HAR.

**Key Contributions:**
*   **Sensor Fusion:** Develops algorithms to effectively fuse data from heterogeneous sensor sources.
*   **Energy Efficiency:** Optimizes data collection and processing to prolong the lifetime of wearable sensors.
*   **Improved HAR Accuracy:** Demonstrates enhanced activity recognition performance through synergistic sensing.

This research was detailed in an [arXiv preprint](/publications/#Mishra2022CoRR_Seeker).
