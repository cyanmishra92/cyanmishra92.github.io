Origin enables on-device intelligence for Human Activity Recognition (HAR) using energy harvesting wireless sensor networks. This project focuses on creating systems that can perform complex HAR tasks directly on sensor nodes powered by ambient energy, eliminating the need for constant battery replacements and cloud connectivity.

**Key Contributions:**
*   **Energy-Efficient HAR:** Designed for ultra-low-power operation on intermittently powered sensor nodes.
*   **On-Device Intelligence:** Performs activity recognition locally, enhancing privacy and reducing latency.
*   **Resilience:** Incorporates mechanisms to handle intermittent power and maintain computational progress.
