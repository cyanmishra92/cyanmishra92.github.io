Uṣa’s is a novel framework designed to enable sustainable and continuous learning on edge servers. As edge devices increasingly perform complex AI tasks, the ability to learn from new data locally without constant retraining from scratch becomes crucial, especially under power constraints.

**Key Contributions:**
*   **Continuous Learning:** Enables edge servers to adapt their models incrementally as new data arrives, improving accuracy and relevance over time.
*   **Sustainability:** Optimized for energy efficiency, allowing operation on resource-constrained edge devices, potentially with intermittent power sources.
*   **Hardware-Software Co-design:** Employs a co-design approach to maximize performance and minimize energy consumption.

This project was presented at **HPCA 2024**.

More details can be found in our [publication](/publications/#Mishra2024HPCA).
