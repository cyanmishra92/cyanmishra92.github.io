ResiRCA (Resilient Energy Harvesting ReRAM Crossbar-Based Accelerator) is an intelligent embedded processor designed for resilience and energy efficiency. It leverages ReRAM crossbar technology for in-memory computing, making it suitable for AI applications on devices powered by unreliable energy harvesting sources.

**Key Contributions:**
*   **ReRAM-based Acceleration:** Utilizes ReRAM for efficient matrix-vector multiplications, a core operation in neural networks.
*   **Energy Harvesting Aware:** Designed to operate effectively with intermittent power supplies.
*   **Resilience:** Incorporates mechanisms to ensure correct operation despite power interruptions and device variations.

This project was presented at **HPCA 2020**.
